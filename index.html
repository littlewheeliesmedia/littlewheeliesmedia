Meet the Little Wheelies

Different jobs.
One amazing team.

Scout, Bear, Asher, and Ollie use curiosity, courage, strength, and smarts to help their friends and turn every day into an adventure.

Scout

The curious leader.

Scout is a friendly school bus who loves learning, exploring, and leading his friends toward the next great adventure.

Bear

Strong. Steady. Kind.

Bear is a dependable dump truck who tackles big jobs, carries heavy loads, and always lends a helping wheel.

Asher

Ready for the rescue.

Asher is a brave fire truck with big energy, a caring heart, and the courage to help whenever trouble comes calling.

Ollie

Clean streets. Clever ideas.

Ollie is a smart, hardworking garbage truck who loves recycling, staying organized, and keeping everything clean and green.
